What is dagger?

Dagger is a simple CLI setup tool written by a developer for other developers. The main idea is that I swap between environments relatively often (Windows to mac to WSL2 to ubuntu, you know the deal). The idea is to make this as seemless as possible each time without having to remember all the packages and commands.

Who is dagger for?

Honestly, dagger seems to be very VERY niche but the great thing is that I want to make dagger flexible and usable for more than just environment setup. Join our discord and contribute to the product in your way.
